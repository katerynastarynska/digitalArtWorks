How it works

Step 1

Place your order and upload your digital photo. Your photo will be customised according to all your wishes. Your photo can be customised with your name or names, date or any text.

Step 2

Pick a size. Your can choose a size that your want and a shape that will suit the best in your interier. Circle, square, rectangle or any custom shape that you like!

Step 3

Choose your background and style. Please specify the style that you would like to be used and the background, if you need any. For a custom background please upload an additional photo that you want to be used for your order.

Step 4

Our graphic designers will create a digital mock-up of your portrait or picture and send it to you for approval. You can ask for modifications and changed if you will need them. When everything will be looked perfect to you, we will send your digital order in high resolution.

Enjoy your order!
